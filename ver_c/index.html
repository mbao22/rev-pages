    Removes 99% of dust and pollen

    Filters more airborne dust and pollen than other purifying fans.¹ Removes over 99%² of airborne allergens like dust and pollen with Blueair's signature HEPASilent filtration technology.

    Cools with patented fan technology

    Patented fan technology delivers a gentle stream of clean, cooling air across a 90-degree range - no oscillation required.

    Simple air quality management

    Set to Auto mode, the fan will auto-increase speed when particle levels rise and return to a lower rate once the air is clean. The air quality indicator (AQI) LED keeps you up to date with 3-colors indicating good, moderate, or polluted based on real-time particle data.

    HEPASilent filtration technology

    A combination of electrostatic and mechanical filtration removes airborne allergens like dust, pollen, and pet dander with low noise and energy consumption.

    Customizable pre-filter colors

    Customize to your space with machine-washable fabric pre-filters available in five colors. Designed to catch large particles to extend the primary filter's life.

    Product Description

    Low energy and noise; ENERGY STAR certified to use less energy than a light bulb³ and cool with noise levels quieter than a whisper on low (25 dB) and less than a normal conversation on high (54 dB)⁴.
